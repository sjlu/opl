Object-Place Learning

This application tracks and records the number and duration of key-presses, minute-by-minute, for a predetermined length of time. Designed for Otto Laboratory, Rutgers University, this application is used in Object-Place Learning (OPL) experiments to quantify behavioral measurements (object exploration).

To start, enter the length of the trial, then simply press and hold keys. Note: only use alphabet keys and do not switch between windows during the trial.
